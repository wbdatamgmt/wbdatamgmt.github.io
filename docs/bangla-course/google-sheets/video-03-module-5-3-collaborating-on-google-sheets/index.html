3. Module 5 - 3 - Collaborating on Google Sheets (বাংলা)

This video is the final installment in the Module 5: Data Analysis with Google Sheets series.

The video focuses on sharing and collaboration features. It guides viewers through the process of sharing Google Sheets files and managing access levels—Viewer, Commenter, and Editor (0:40–4:30).

A significant portion of the video is dedicated to the Protect Sheets and Ranges feature (4:35–10:30). The instructor demonstrates how to:

  • Protect entire sheets to prevent unauthorized changes to sensitive data (6:15–7:00).
  • Apply granular protection to specific cell ranges, allowing collaborators to input data in designated areas while keeping structural or calculated elements secure (7:45–9:30).

Additionally, the video covers how to invite specific collaborators via email versus sharing a link (10:40–11:55), ensuring you can control exactly who has permission to edit your work.
