Module 4 - 3 - Adding Images and Videos

Jun 6, 2023

This video provides a practical guide on how to enhance Google Forms by integrating images and videos (0:02-0:15).

Key highlights include:

  • Adding Media Elements: Demonstrates how to insert videos directly from YouTube (0:36-0:49) or upload images from your computer or other sources (0:26-0:32).
  • Section Management: Explains how to use these media elements to break up a form into smaller, organized sections, including adding custom titles and descriptions (1:51-2:21).
  • Interactive Questions: Shows how to attach images directly to specific questions, allowing for more engaging data collection (2:46-3:50).

The tutorial concludes by noting that these features are particularly useful for creating larger, more complex forms, and previews upcoming lessons on managing responses and sharing settings (4:10-4:28).
