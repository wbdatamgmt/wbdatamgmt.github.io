Data Collection with Google Forms Assessment

This assessment is optional but highly recommended. Javascript-based answer checking will be added later.

  1. Which of the following is the primary purpose of Google Forms? a. To edit images b. To create presentations X. To collect and analyse data through surveys or quizzes d. To manage spreadsheets

  2. What type of question in Google Forms allows respondents to select multiple answers? a. Short answer X. Checkboxes c. Paragraph d. Linear scale

  3. How can you add a new question to a Google Form? X. By clicking the plus (+) button on the right side of the form b. By pressing the Enter key c. By selecting File > New Question d. By right-clicking on the form background

  4. Which question type would you use to ask respondents to rate their satisfaction on a scale from 1 to 5? a. Multiple choice X. Linear scale c. Dropdown d. Paragraph

  5. How do you change the theme or colour of your Google Form? a. By selecting View > Change Theme X. By clicking the ‘Customise theme’ icon at the top of the form c. By right-clicking on the form title d. By choosing Insert > Theme

  6. Which feature in Google Forms allows you to automatically collect respondents’ email addresses? X. By turning on the “Collect email addresses” option in the Settings menu b. By adding an email question manually c. By sharing the form via email d. By requiring a login to access the form

  7. What is the purpose of the Responses tab in Google Forms? a. To add more questions b. To change the form’s theme X. To view and analyse the data submitted by respondents d. To share the form link

  8. Which question type in Google Forms allows you to provide a list of options where only one answer can be selected? X. Multiple choice b. Checkboxes c. Paragraph d. Dropdown

  9. How can you make a Google Form a quiz? a. By adding a new section b. By adding a “Quiz” question type X. By enabling the “Make this a quiz” option in the Settings menu d. By changing the form’s theme

  10. How can you share your Google Form with others? a. By copying and pasting the form’s URL X. By clicking the “Send” button and choosing a sharing method c. By printing the form and distributing it d. By exporting the form as a PDF
