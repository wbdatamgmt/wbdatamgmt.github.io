Module 4 - 5 - Responses, Quizzes and Settings

Jun 6, 2023

This video shows how to view responses in a Google Form. It also shows how to create a quiz using a Google Form and covers other settings.

The video provides as a comprehensive guide to managing Google Forms once they have been distributed. Key topics covered include:

  • Analyzing Responses: How to view and interpret collected data within the Responses tab, including automatic charts and exporting data to Google Sheets (0:00 - 5:42).
  • Managing Form Availability: Utilizing the toggle switch to stop accepting responses, setting up email notifications, and handling result downloads (3:44 - 5:42).
  • Creating Quizzes: How to enable the quiz feature, assign point values to questions, and provide automated feedback with links or videos (5:46 - 9:08).
  • Form Settings: Configuring email collection, response editing, limiting submissions, and presentation options like progress bars or autosave (9:11 - 14:47).
  • Sharing & Collaboration: How to generate shareable links and invite collaborators to manage forms collectively (14:58 - 17:49).
