Creating and Sharing a Google Form 🔗

In this assignment, you will create a Google Form to collect basic personal information from your colleagues. This will help you practice using different question types, customising the form’s appearance, and analysing responses.

Step 1: Create a New Google Form 🔗

  • Open Google Forms by going to forms.google.com.
  • Click on the + Blank option to create a new form.

Step 2: Name Your Form and Add a Description 🔗

  • At the top of the form, give your form a title, such as “Office Personnel Information”.
  • In the description box below the title, briefly explain the purpose of the form, for example: “Please provide your basic information for our office records.”

Step 3: Add Questions to Your Form 🔗

  • Name: Add a Short answer question type asking for the respondent’s name.
  • Qualifications: Add a Multiple choice or Checkboxes question type to allow respondents to select their qualifications, such as BA, BSc, MA etc.
  • Background: Use a Paragraph question type to let respondents write about their professional background.
  • Interests: Use a Checkboxes question type to allow respondents to select their areas of interest and hobbies.
  • Contact Information: Add a Short answer question type for their email address and phone number.
  • You can add more questions as per your requirement.

Step 4: Customise the Form’s Appearance 🔗

  • Click on the Palette icon at the top right of the screen to change the form’s theme.
  • Choose a colour scheme or image that aligns with your office’s branding or personal preferences.
  • Experiment with different fonts and background colours to make your form visually appealing.

Step 5: Add an Image or Video (Optional) 🔗

  • To add an image, click the Image icon in any question or section header and upload an image.
  • To add a video, click the Video icon and search for a YouTube video or paste a video link.

Step 6: Explore Advanced Question Types (Optional) 🔗

  • Experiment with advanced question types like Linear scale, Date, or File upload.

Step 7: Set Up Form Settings 🔗

  • Click on the Settings gear icon at the top of the form.
  • Under the Responses section, choose whether to collect email addresses, allow editing after submission, or restrict responses to one per person.

Step 8: Share the Form with Colleagues 🔗

  • Click on the Send button at the top right of the screen.
  • Choose the Link option to get a shareable link.
  • Copy the link and share it with your colleagues via email or any communication platform.

Step 9: Collect and Explore Responses 🔗

  • Wait for your colleagues to fill out the form.
  • Check the Responses tab to view and analyse the collected data.
  • Explore the summary of responses, individual responses, and the option to export data to Google Sheets for further analysis.
