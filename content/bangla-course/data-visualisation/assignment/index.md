## Creating Charts from Real Data

In this assignment, you will download real school infrastructure data from the UDISE+ portal, summarise it, and create a pie chart in Excel.

1. Go to the [UDISE+ Report Dashboard](https://dashboard.udiseplus.gov.in/udiseplus-archive/#/reportDashboard/sReport).

2. Find and click on the report **"Number of Schools by Availability of Infrastructure and Facilities, School Management and School Category"** (report ID: 3013).

3. Select latest year, West Bengal state, and District-wise in the district option.

4. Download the data by clicking on the green Excel icon on the top right of the report card.

5. After downloading the file, open it in MS Excel.

6. Add a filter to the data, as you may have learnt in Module 1.

7. Filter the Location column and get the data for your district only.

8. Filter further the School Management column to get information on Department of Education schools.

9. Select this entire data and paste into a new sheet, call it **TABLE 1**. This is all infrastructure related information for your district and schools under SED.

10. Add a sum formula at the end of your TABLE 1 and find the total number of schools in each column.

11. Find how many schools have a Playground in total and how many do not have a playground and create a separate summary table for this. Call it **TABLE 2**.

12. TABLE 2 should look like the exhibit below:

![Table 2 example](/mod-3-assigment-table.png)

13. Select the entire TABLE 2 and click on **Insert** in the menu bar, then select **Recommended Charts**.

14. Make a pie chart.

15. After the pie chart is created, select the chart, go to **Chart Design**, then **Add Chart Element**. Add title, add legend, add labels.

16. The chart should look like below:

![Pie chart example](/mod-3-assigment-pie.png)

17. Feel free to change colours and experiment with different elements of the chart.

18. What are the good practices you followed in the chart?

19. Try downloading other indicators from the UDISE+ portal and using relevant data to create a line chart and bar chart as well.

20. What are some of the other charts you see on Excel? Have you tried them too?
